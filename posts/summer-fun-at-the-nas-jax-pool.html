Summer Fun at the NAS Jax Pool!

📍 NAS Jax Pool - Jacksonville, FL

Watch on YouTube →

We had an absolute blast at the NAS Jacksonville MWR pool this week! There is no better way to beat the Florida heat than spending the day poolside, and the kids were thrilled to have finished their annual swim test compliance item.


With that milestone checked off, they had full clearance to enjoy all the fun features the pool has to offer. They spent most of the day testing their skills on the Tarzan swing! It’s easily the favorite part of their visit.


If you’re planning to head out, here are the hours we noted on June 12th:

  • Mon–Fri: 6 AM – 6 PM

  • Sat, Sun & Holidays: 10 AM – 4 PM


Don’t forget to check the official NAS Jax MWR page for any specific facility updates before you go. See you at the pool!
