Hoops and Highlights: A Week at the NAS Jax Youth Activities Center

📍 Youth Center

Last week was an action-packed one for the kids, as they spent their days on base at the NAS Jax Youth Activities Center. It was a fantastic experience for them, and I’m so glad we could take advantage of the programs run by NavyMWR Jacksonville.

The highlight of the week was definitely their basketball camp. They had a blast working on their game, and it was a special treat for them to have their camp hosted by former NBA player Reggie Evans! It was great to see them learning from someone with his professional experience.
